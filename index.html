Discover the Rich Heritage of Indigenous Communities

Many indigenous tribes in the Philippines have traditionally lived in mountainous regions, preserving their customs, beliefs, and way of life for generations.

About

Indigenous Peoples (IPs) are the original inhabitants of different regions of the Philippines. Many communities continue to protect their language, traditions, and ancestral lands.

Some Indigenous Tribes

Manobo

The Manobo people are among the largest indigenous groups in Mindanao. Many communities have traditionally lived in forested and mountainous areas.

Higaonon

The Higaonon are known for their strong cultural traditions, respect for nature, and ancestral lands in Northern Mindanao.

T'boli

The T'boli are famous for their colorful clothing, traditional music, and T'nalak weaving.

B'laan

The B'laan people are recognized for their brasswork, embroidery, and unique cultural heritage.

Culture & Traditions

  • Traditional dances and music
  • Handwoven textiles
  • Respect for nature and ancestral lands
  • Oral storytelling and folklore
  • Community rituals and ceremonies
